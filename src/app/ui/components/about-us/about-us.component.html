About Tüm Trading

What did we set out for?

Tüm Trading was founded by a group of people with trading experience in different geographies. Providing consultancy services to the leading companies of the business world, Tüm Trading aims to ensure the successful conclusion of all regional and global commercial operations with its knowledgeable, talented, and experienced human resources.

Our Expertise

Our specialists are expected to possess strong analytical skills, efficient industrial manufacturing, and access to a viewpoint that examines stagnation's root causes and promotes transformation. Our goal is to assist businesses in realizing their full potential and emerging as industry leaders by providing a broad model of collaboration across all organizational levels in the commercial enterprises they support.

Quality Policy

Tüm Trading bases all of its operations on "quality-oriented" labor. In keeping with this approach:

  • Advancing company owners and entrepreneurs to a position of leadership and innovation in their industries
  • To guarantee the contentment of every partner in the solution, particularly our staff
  • To direct sustainable development, efficient resource use, and ongoing progress
  • To encourage and support innovation and entrepreneurship
  • To conduct business in compliance with applicable laws and worldwide management standards

Our Business Ethics

A. Honesty

Our main principles in all of our commercial dealings and interpersonal interactions are honesty and integrity. We conduct ourselves with honesty and integrity toward all of our stakeholders, including our employees.

B. Privacy

Confidential and proprietary information comprises trade secrets, financial and other information that hasn't been made public yet, information about personnel's personal rights, and information that falls under the purview of "confidentiality agreements" signed with third parties. It also includes information that belongs to Tüm Group Companies that might put them at a competitive disadvantage. As staff members of Tüm Group, we take precautions to safeguard the personal information of our clients, partners, and other pertinent parties.

It is absolutely unacceptable for us to obtain any financial benefit by disclosing any private information owned by Tüm Group Companies, including trading shares on stock markets. We do not take with us any confidential documents, projects, policies, or other information that we are required to have because of our work when we leave our organization.

C. Conflict of Interest

We aim to avoid conflicts of interest as Tüm Group employees. Making the most of our current mission: We do not personally gain anything from people or companies that we do business with, either directly or through family or relatives.

At Tüm Group Companies, we do not engage in business activities based on additional financial interests.

We use these techniques in the event of a possible conflict of interest when we think that morally and legally sound measures can safeguard the interests of concerned parties. We seek advice from the Ethics Committee, the Human Resources Department, or our boss whenever we are doubtful.

D. Our Responsibilities

In addition to our legal obligations, we make sure to meet our following responsibilities towards our competitors, society, mankind, and our firm. These responsibilities include those towards our customers, employees, shareholders, suppliers, and business partners.

1. Our Legal Responsibilities

We conduct all of our present business both domestically and internationally in accordance with the rules of the Republic of Turkey and international law. We also promptly and accurately supply legal regulatory bodies and organizations with information that is clear, comprehensive, and correct.

2. Our Responsibilities Towards Our Customers

We have a proactive strategy that prioritizes client satisfaction and allows us to promptly and accurately address our clients' needs and requests. We meet our deadlines and provide our services as agreed upon; we deal with our clients in accordance with the principles of decency, fairness, equality, and civility.

3. Our Responsibilities Towards Employees

We make certain that workers' personal rights are appropriately and completely utilized. We treat each employee fairly and honestly, and we're dedicated to providing a safe, healthy, and nondiscriminatory work environment. We put in the required effort to help our employees grow personally, encourage them to volunteer for suitable social activities while maintaining a sense of social duty, and uphold the separation of work and personal life.

4. Our Responsibilities Towards Our Partners

By prioritizing the Tüm Group's continued existence and adhering to the objective of adding value for our partners: We steer clear of needless or uncontrollable risks and strive for long-term financial success.

We perform within the framework of financial discipline and accountability, and we manage our company's resources and assets, as well as our working time, with an awareness of efficiency and savings. We invest in sectors with growth potential and that will yield the maximum return on the linked resource in an effort to strengthen our competitiveness.

5. Our Responsibilities Towards Our Suppliers/Business Partners

As one would expect from a good client, we behave honestly and professionally and take reasonable steps to ensure that our responsibilities are met on schedule. We take great effort to safeguard the private information of our business partners as well as the individuals and companies with whom we do business.

6. Our Responsibilities Towards Our Competitors

We compete effectively only in areas that are legal and ethical, avoiding unfair competition. We support efforts to ensure the targeted competitive structure within the society.

7. Our Responsibilities Towards Society and Humanity

We place a great deal of importance on the preservation of democracy, human rights, and the environment; on charitable work and education; and on the eradication of crime and corruption. We strive to participate in non-governmental organizations, public interest services, and relevant actions on these concerns because we recognize our responsibility as good citizens and act sensitively as pioneers in social issues. We respect the customs and cultures of both Turkey and the nations in which we do foreign initiatives.

Health and Safety Policy

Tüm Trading strives to offer a safe and healthy working environment for all of its operations. In keeping with this objective, we commit to:

  • Creating a healthy workforce and population
  • Implementing essential procedures for preventing workplace accidents and occupational ailments
  • Carrying out risk assessments with staff participation to attain suitable risk levels
  • Ensuring participation from all organizational levels in enhancing occupational health and safety procedures
  • Fostering a culture of workplace health and safety
  • Complying with applicable laws and regulations for occupational health and safety
  • Taking a proactive stance toward occupational health and safety while boosting productivity
  • Maintaining training programs for staff, visitors, interns, and subcontractor personnel
